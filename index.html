Geographical Location of Japan

Japan is geographically part of Northeast Asia, facing the Pacific Ocean to the east and bordered by the Sea of Japan, the Korea Strait, and the East China Sea to the west, as well as the warm southwestern part of the Eurasian continent. It becomes colder as you go north. In particular, during winter, the northwestern monsoon brings a lot of snow to the Sea of Japan side. On the other hand, the Pacific side is often characterized by dry and clear weather, known as "winter sunshine."
